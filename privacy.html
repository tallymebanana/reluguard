Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 January 2026

ReluGuard (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use personal information when you visit reluguard.vercel.app (the “Site”).

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

When you submit a form or begin a purchase journey, you may provide:

  • Email address
  • Organisation name (optional)
  • Template customisation inputs you choose (e.g., risk appetite, AI tools in scope, data sensitivity focus)
  • Any message you send via the contact form (e.g., invoice/PO requests)

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store your card details. Stripe receives payment and billing information directly and processes it under its own privacy policy.

Technical information (limited)

When you use the Site, limited technical information may be processed for security and abuse prevention (for example, IP address, timestamps, and user agent via standard server logs and form submissions).

2. Advertising and tracking

We may run ads on platforms such as Google and LinkedIn to direct people to this Site. These platforms may use their own cookies and tracking technologies on their services.

On this Site, we do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or behavioural advertising cookies. We do not run Meta/LinkedIn “Insight Tags”, Google Analytics, or similar tracking scripts.

If you arrive via an ad, the link may include campaign parameters (e.g., utm_source, utm_campaign). If present, we may process these parameters in a limited way to understand which campaigns drive enquiries or purchases. We do not use them to build behavioural profiles or track you across other websites.

3. How we use your information

  • Respond to enquiries (including invoice/PO requests)
  • Log purchase intent and form submissions (operational record-keeping)
  • Deliver the purchased template by email
  • Provide customer support (e.g., resend delivery email if needed)
  • Prevent misuse, spam, or fraud

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Sharing your information

We share personal information only with service providers necessary to operate the Site, such as:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Vercel (hosting and server logs)
  • Resend (email delivery)

We may also share information where required by law or to protect our rights and prevent fraud.

5. Legal basis (EU/EEA)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, we process personal data based on:

  • Contract (to deliver the product you purchase)
  • Legitimate interests (to respond to enquiries, operate the Site, and prevent abuse)
  • Legal obligations (where applicable for accounting/tax records)

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil purchases and enquiries, operate the Site, meet legal/accounting obligations, and resolve disputes.

7. Your rights (EU/EEA)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can also request data portability where applicable.

8. Cookies

The Site does not use analytics or advertising cookies. Essential cookies used by third-party services (such as Stripe during checkout) are subject to those providers’ policies.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on the Site.
